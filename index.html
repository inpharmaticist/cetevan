The Digital Mattress Problem

You moved your wealth out of the banking system for a reason. But if your Bitcoin is sitting on an exchange, or stored on a single hardware wallet in your desk drawer, you haven't eliminated risk—you've just changed its shape.

For those of us who remember safety deposit boxes and bond certificates, Bitcoin can feel like stuffing cash in a digital mattress. You know it's valuable. You know it needs protection. But the tools feel foreign, and the stakes are high.

The 5-Minute Security Audit

Check your current setup:

  • If your house/devices were destroyed, could you recover your bitcoin?
  • Could your loved one(s) access the funds without help after you're gone?
  • Do you have backups in multiple locations?
  • Is your recovery phrase removed from computers/phone/cloud?
  • Have you done a recovery test this year?
A "NO" means you're at risk of losing your bitcoin.
