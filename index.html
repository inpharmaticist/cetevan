The 5-Minute Security Audit

Check your current setup:

  • If your house/devices were destroyed, could you recover your bitcoin?
  • Could your loved one(s) access the funds without help after you're gone?
  • Do you have backups in multiple locations?
  • Is your recovery phrase removed from computers/phone/cloud?
  • Have you done a recovery test this year?
A "NO" means you're at risk of losing your bitcoin.
